Rivers In Idaho

Discover Idaho's ultimate white-water adventure! From epic rapids to breathtaking wilderness, Idaho's rivers offer some of the most exhilarating rafting experiences in the country.

Snake River

The Snake River is one of Idaho's top white-water rafting destinations, with sections that offer rapids for all skill levels amidst stunning natural landscapes. Here, rafters encounter exhilarating Class III and IV rapids as they journey through the steep, rocky walls of the canyon, which at some points reaches depths of over a mile.

Salmon River

The Salmon River is one of Idaho's premier white-water rafting rivers, known for its thrilling rapids and remote, pristine wilderness. Flowing through the Frank Church-River of No Return Wilderness, it offers multi-day rafting trips with a mix of Class III and IV rapids that appeal to intermediate and experienced rafters.

Payette River

The Payette River in Idaho is a favorite for white-water rafting, known for its accessibility, scenic beauty, and diverse range of rapids. Located near Boise, the river has sections suitable for all experience levels, from gentle Class II rapids to thrilling Class IV and V runs, making it ideal for both beginners and seasoned rafters.
